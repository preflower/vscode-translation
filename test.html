test/tɛst/CET4 TEM4(testing,tested,ttests)

  • 1. 

    V-TtWhen you test something, you try it, for example, by touching it or using it for a short time, in order to find out what it is, what condition it is in, or how well it works. 试验

    例：

    Either measure the temperature with a thermometer or test the water with your wrist.

    要么用温度计测量水温，要么用你的手腕来试水温。

  • 2. 

    N-COUNTtA test is a deliberate action or experiment to find out how well something works. 试验

    例：

    ...the banning of nuclear tests.

    …对核试验的禁止。

  • 3. 

    V-TtIf you test someone, you ask them questions or tell them to perform certain actions in order to find out how much they know about a subject or how well they are able to do something. 测试

    例：

    There was a time when each teacher spent an hour, one day a week, testing students in every subject.

    曾有一段时间，每个老师每周都花一小时来测验学生的各门功课。

  • 4. 

    N-COUNTtA test is a series of questions that you must answer or actions that you must perform in order to show how much you know about a subject or how well you are able to do something. 测试

    例：

    Out of a total of 25 students only 15 passed the test.

    所有25个学生中只有15个通过了测试。

    从第一天起，鲁道夫就在考验我，看我会不会给他泡茶，给他拿可乐。

 test, experiment, trial, try

这组词都有“试验”的意思，其区别是：

test普通用词，含义广，指用科学方法对某物质进行测试以估价其性质或效能等。

experiment多指用科学方法在实验室内进行较系统的操作实验以验证、解释或说明某一理论、定理或某一观点等。

trial指为观察、研究某事物以区别其真伪、优劣或效果等而进行较长时间的试验或试用过程。

try普通用词，多用于口语或非正式场合，指试一试。

以上来源于网络

 examination, test, quiz, exam

这组词都有“考试”的意思，其区别是：

examination通常指为检查学生知识或能力而举行的正式考试，有时也指临时考试。

test多指有具体目的的临时性考试或小考。

quiz指时间较短，随时进行的口头或书面的非正式测验。

exam系examination的缩略形式，口语用词，通俗。
